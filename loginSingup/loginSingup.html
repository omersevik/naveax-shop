Safety Considerations for Using Headphones

Awareness of Ambient Noise:

When using headphones with active noise-cancellation (ANC) technology, your awareness of the environment may decrease. In traffic, while walking, or in outdoor settings, disable ANC to ensure you can hear important ambient sounds.

Using Headphones While Driving:

Wearing headphones while driving or cycling can block critical environmental sounds, increasing the risk of accidents. Avoid using headphones in these situations to maintain full awareness.

Wired Headphones:

Wired headphones pose a risk of snagging during sudden movements. Opt for wireless models when exercising or performing activities that involve frequent motion to minimize this hazard.

Headphones at Night:

Sleeping with headphones can cause discomfort and pressure on your ears, and it may prevent you from waking up in emergencies. Consider alternatives like speakers for nighttime use.
